1 Overview

This supplement reports the most useful numerical summaries from the simulations.
Across tables, the main quantities are:

  • Observed d: mean observed NCA effect size across replications.
  • SD(d): standard deviation of the observed NCA effect size across replications.
  • Mean r(X,Y): mean observed correlation between X and Y across replications.
  • X skew and Y skew: mean observed skewness of the analyzed X and Y scores across replications.
  • Delta from latent base: mean difference between the observed effect and the latent-reference NCA effect from the full latent sample in the same design cell.
  • Delta from latent selected: mean difference between the observed effect and the condition-specific latent reference after selection. This column is only used for the range-restriction block.
